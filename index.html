Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you offer? +

We offer comprehensive accounting services including tax planning and preparation, bookkeeping, audit services, business consulting, and financial planning. Our services cater to individuals, small businesses, and large corporations.

How much do your services cost? +

Our pricing varies based on the complexity and scope of services required. We offer competitive rates and transparent pricing. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.

Do you work with both businesses and individuals? +

Yes! We serve a diverse client base including individual taxpayers, small businesses, startups, and large corporations. Our team has expertise across all client types and industries.

How do I get started? +

Getting started is easy! Simply schedule a free consultation through our website or call us directly. We'll discuss your needs, explain our services, and create a customized plan for your financial success.

What documents do I need for tax preparation? +

Typically, you'll need W-2s, 1099s, receipts for deductions, investment statements, and records of income and expenses. We'll provide you with a comprehensive checklist during your consultation to ensure we have everything needed.
